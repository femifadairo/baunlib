Baun Digital Library

Baun Digital Library (BauLib) is a battery-powered educational learning library designed to deliver curated content to remote, offline and off-grid locations. It helps students in underserved communities develop relevant Internet-ready skills.

Great Catalog

Access digital library contents without an internet connection, eliminating the need to visit a physical location.

Vast Content

Baun Digital library can store a vast amount of content, including PDFs, video, and more. This content can include books, articles, journals, multimedia, and archival materials.

Multimedia Content

Access a wide range of media types, including PDFs, videos, Docx, and interactive multimedia.

Open Access

Some digital libraries focus on open access content, making scholarly and educational materials freely available to the public.
